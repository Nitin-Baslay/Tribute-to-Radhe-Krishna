Radhe Krishna

With madhura bhakti or sweet devotion, Radharani achieved atmasamyogam or union with the Divine. When we call Him Radhakrishna, we acknowledge this love-union.

It is believed that Krishna enchants the world, but Radha enchants even him. Therefore, she is the supreme goddess of all and together they are called as Radha-Krishna. Whenever we talk about Radha Krishna (we never say Radha without Krishna), who are just so darn in love with each other that both their names have combined into one, we just have one thing in our head - their love story. Their true love and dedication have kept them alive in our hearts, their love story is hymned with praise for ages.
